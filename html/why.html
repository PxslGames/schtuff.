Why does Schtuff exist?

Schtuff exists for two main reasons:

1. To create a cool and enjoyable website for everyone

I wanted to build something fun that anyone could enjoy. A place full of random and quirky things to explore. This website is meant to be a place where you can discover little weird creations, big or small, all in one place!

2. To gather all my "micro-websites" in one place

I have way too many ideas for small web projects, like a username generator or a random quiz. These ideas are often too small to stand on their own as full websites. So, I thought, "Why not just merge them all into one place?" That's where Schtuff comes in!

That’s also why Schtuff is so random — whatever silly, funny, or cool ideas for web projects I get, I just throw them here! It’s a little chaotic, but that’s the fun of it!

I really hope you like my website and enjoy the randomness! Thanks for visiting!
